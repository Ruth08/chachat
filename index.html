What is Chat Support

An online customer service software with live support, help desk software, and web analytics capabilities. It was first launched in 2002 and is currently developed and offered in a SaaS (software as a service) business model by LiveChat Software. Companies use LiveChat as a single point of contact to manage from one software all customer service and online sales activities that normally are provided using different channels (chat, email and social media) and multiple tools.Chat Support is used by over 21,000 paid customers from 118 countries.
